Our Mission

One Less Worry Period (OLWP) is committed to providing all birthing people with love, dignity, and respect during this transformative time in their lives. By providing support, perspective, trust, advocacy, and a sense of community, OLWP promises to help birthing people have one less worry during this beautiful and critical time. We put the needs of birthing people first.
